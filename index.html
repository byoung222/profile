    My Interests

    Machine Learning, biology, artificial intelligence, computer science, and entrepreneurship.

    As of late, I've been working on writing an application called Aggregate, a digital platform that bridges the gap between producers and consumers in local communities. This summer, I participated in the Break Through Tech AI program, where I took a Machine Learning Foundations course at Cornell University, began working on a multi-label regression model for bird species encounter rates, and receiving AI mentorship. I am currently pursuing Information Science and Statistics at Cornell University. Some of my favorite activities include learning about artificial intelligence and entrepreneurship, both inside and outside of the classroom. At school, I'm exploring some of my research interests on the Food Systems and Global Change team as a Livestock and Crop Emissions Data Research Assistant, and as a AI Studio Fellow at Mila this semester. You can find me coding, writing (on my Medium and Substack), reading, or painting.
