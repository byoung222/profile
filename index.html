    My Interests

    Machine Learning, biology, artificial intelligence, computer science, and entrepreneurship.

    As of late, I've been working on writing an application called Aggregate, a digital platform that bridges the gap between producers and consumers in local communities. My co-founder and I plan to launch our business soon (within the next month or so). So far, we've won third place at the Panasci Business Plan Competition, the Raymond von Dran iPrize, and a Social Impact Award. This summer, I'll be participating in the Break Through Tech AI program, where I'll be taking Machine Learning courses, working on industry projects, and receiving AI mentorship. I am currently pursuing a dual degree in Applied Data Analytics and Business Management at Syracuse University. Some of my favorite activities include learning about artificial intelligence and entrepreneurship, both inside and outside of the classroom. At school, I'm exploring some of my research interests on the AI research team of the NEXIS Lab, and as a new undergraduate research assistant in the Bioacoustic and Behavioral Ecology Lab this semester. You can find me coding, writing (on my Medium and Substack), reading, or painting.
